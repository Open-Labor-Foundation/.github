# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Open Labor Foundation a respectful, collaborative experience for everyone — regardless of background, experience level, or profession.

## Our standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Treat all participants with respect
- Welcome contributions from people of all skill levels and backgrounds
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Dismissing contributions based on who someone is rather than what they submitted
- Personal attacks or derogatory language

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **conduct@openlabor.foundation**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers will take appropriate action, up to and including permanent removal from the community.
